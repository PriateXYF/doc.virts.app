单词替换

B

there-be 句型中 be 的单词替换。

  • exist
  • appear
  • seem
  • remain
  • keep
  • prove
  • become
  • get

C

children 的单词替换。

  • kids 小孩子
  • offspring 子女 子孙后代
  • descendants 子女 子孙后代

E

explain 的单词替换。

  • account for

I

idea 的单词替换。

  • viewpoint
  • outlook
  • perspective
  • point of view
  • argument

L

lead to 的单词替换。

  • give rise to
  • contribute to
  • be responsible for

M

more and more 的单词替换。

  • an increasing number of

many 的单词替换。 可数 / 不可数 / 随意

  • a sea of
  • a host of
  • a multitude of
  • an army of
  • a crowd of
  • various

以上单词后面只能 + 复数名词(可数名词)

  • immense amounts of

以上单词后面只能 + 不可数名词

  • numerous
  • innumerable
  • abundant
  • plentiful

以上单词后面随便加。

N

not 的单词替换。

  • hardly
  • seldom
  • never
  • by no means
  • under no circumstances 在任何情况下绝不
  • fail to

P

parents 的单词替换。

  • the elderly / elders
  • senior citizens
  • superiors

we / us / people 的单词替换。

  • the public
  • the folks
  • the masses
  • human beings
  • private individuals

people 的 排比替换 。

  • teenagers and youngsters
  • youngsters on campus
  • all children and adults
  • men and women in all walks
  • experts and professors

R

reasons 的单词替换。

  • causes
  • factors
  • elements
  • grounds
  • arguments

S

should 的单词替换。

  • be supposed to
  • ought to
  • be obliged to 带强制意味

superstars 的单词替换。

  • celebrities

serious 的单词替换。

  • grave 坟墓 严重的
  • severe
  • fearful
  • fatal
  • grievous
  • critical
  • be of great severity

T

think 的单词替换。

  • contend
  • assume
  • claim
  • presume
  • insist
  • hold
  • argue
  • maintain
  • assert
  • reckon
  • be of the opinion that
  • have been convinced that
  • cling to the perspective

I think 的 短语替换 。

  • I would contend that
  • I presumed that
  • It is reasonable to assume that
  • It keeps my perspective that

V

very 的单词替换。

作文中任何一个 形容词 / 副词 前都可以加 very 。

  • pretty
  • quite
  • rather
  • distinctly
  • remarkable
  • especially
  • extremely
  • enormously
  • outstandingly
  • exceedingly
  • extraordinarily
  • incredible
  • strikingly
  • more than
